# Interview Prep Message

> **Tip:** Use this when you have an interview for a role you did **not** draft a cover letter for in this project. Start a **new chat** in your project/notebook and paste the message below.

Paste this into your project chat to prep for an interview.

---

Interview prep only — I didn't write a cover letter for this role here.

Job posting: [paste the job description or link]
Org website: [link to the organization's about/mission page, optional]
Interview details: [round, format (phone / video / panel), interviewer role(s), date — optional]

Before the questions, work through this with me in order:

1. **Confirm the job details.** Pull the key requirements, responsibilities, and must-haves out of the posting. Flag anything ambiguous and ask me to fill the gaps.
2. **Fit check.** Compare the posting against my resume: strong-fit areas, gaps or stretches, and an honest verdict.
3. **Gather details.** Ask me 2-4 targeted questions to surface relevant stories and accomplishments for the likely interview topics — or let me upload a story bank if I have one.
4. **Interview prep.** Then run the prep. For every talking point, label how strongly my own materials support it — **Strong** (clear evidence), **Moderate** (partial or related evidence), or **Thin** (needs a real example I should prepare) — so I know what's solid and what I need to shore up.

Walk me through it.

---

## Tips

- This reuses the resume, writing samples, and voice fingerprint already in your project — no need to repeat them.
- If the job posting is a URL and the fetch fails, paste the text directly.
- **Thin** talking points are your homework: prep a concrete example before the interview.
